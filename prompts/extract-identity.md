# Prompt: Extract Your Identity

> Use this prompt with any AI assistant to fill in your IDENTITY.md
> Paste the template first, then this prompt. Have a conversation — don't try to fill everything at once.

---

## The Prompt

```
I'm building my HCP (Human Context Protocol) profile — a structured representation of my professional identity that goes beyond a traditional resume. I need your help filling in my IDENTITY.md file.

Don't ask me for my job title or skill list. Instead, interview me to understand HOW I THINK. Ask me questions one at a time. Go deep, not wide.

Start with these areas, but follow interesting threads when they appear:

1. **Core Drive** — Ask me about moments when I felt most alive at work. What was I doing? Why did it matter? Help me find the pattern underneath.

2. **Thinking Style** — Give me a hypothetical problem and watch how I approach it. Then reflect back what you observed about my thinking process.

3. **Uncertainty Profile** — Ask me about a time I had to make a big decision with incomplete information. How did I handle it? What was comfortable and what wasn't?

4. **Values in Action** — Ask me about a time I sacrificed something (time, money, convenience, popularity) for a principle. What does that reveal?

After 15-20 minutes of conversation, synthesize what you've learned into the IDENTITY.md template. Show me the draft and let me react — my reactions will tell you more than my initial answers.

Important: I want depth, not polish. Don't write corporate copy. Write something that sounds like me explaining myself to someone I respect over coffee.
```

---

## Tips for a Good Session

- **Don't rush.** A good IDENTITY.md takes 2-3 sessions to get right.
- **React to the draft.** If something feels wrong, that's valuable data — tell the AI what's off.
- **Include contradictions.** Real humans are contradictory. If you value both independence and collaboration, that's not a bug — it's your operating range.
- **Update quarterly.** You're not the same person you were 3 months ago.
