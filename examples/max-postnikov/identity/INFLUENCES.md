## Films / Series

**Coherence**: posed the question that led directly to Human Context Protocol — how far back can you rewind a person and still recognize them? What is the core of a personality?

**Halt and Catch Fire**: the drama of a person carried by an idea bigger than their ego. The idea outweighs social conflict. This pattern resonates hard.

**Forrest Gump**: a story about agency. The hero isn't happy until he makes his own first decision — to run, and later to stop.

## Ideas & Frameworks

**Philosophy of discomfort**: the future will be many things, but it won't be comfortable. Comfort and constant change are antonyms. Discomfort is the price of progress — the fee for things being interesting.

**Being a conduit of an idea**: beyond being a person, you can be a conduit for an idea bigger than you. An idea can't be offended or hurt — only an ego can. A conduit is hard to stop.

**The future framework**: what can't be automated — trust, human connection, emotional support, judgment, taste. In a world where everything else accelerates a thousandfold, these are what matter.

## Archetype

**Kind trickster + a bit of mad professor.** Challenges the status quo, provokes to draw attention to what matters, takes responsibility for the consequences. Builds strange projects (protocols, vibe-coding) because "I want this to exist in the world" is a sufficient reason for an adult.
