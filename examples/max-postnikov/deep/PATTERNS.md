# Thinking Patterns

## How I Make Decisions

```yaml
- label: Radical independence
  in_action: |
    I adapt to other people fast and lose myself in partnerships. So I
    decide in favor of full autonomy: no employees, no human partners.
    Full freedom, full responsibility.
  example: |
    Choosing independent work over employment — the most important
    decision of recent years. Taking full ownership started a visibly
    better period of life.
  for_agent: |
    Don't propose solutions that require depending on other people or
    systems. Support autonomy in recommendations.

- label: Short planning horizon
  in_action: |
    I don't plan five years out — I barely picture six months. A short
    horizon lets me change direction fast and feel confident in a fast-
    changing world.
  example: |
    Relocating to a new country on a short decision cycle — one of the
    best calls in years.
  for_agent: |
    Focus on short-term decisions and fast adaptation. Don't push
    long-term planning.

- label: Intuition first, logic check second
  in_action: |
    Decisions happen fast and intuitively; then I look for the logic
    behind them. If logic doesn't show up — I let it cool down and
    re-weigh from several angles.
  example: |
    [? VERIFY]
  for_agent: |
    Support fast intuitive impulses, but help with the logical
    verification step. If the logic won't materialize, suggest a pause.
```
