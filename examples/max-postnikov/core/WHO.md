# Who I Am

## Role
A conduit of knowledge and meaning for people.

## Core Drive
I'm most myself when I share what matters to me and watch it start to matter to others. Second drive: following curiosity without a map — exploring the new and changing in the process.

## Values

```yaml
- label: Transmitting meaning
  in_action: |
    Public speaking, podcasts, teaching, mentoring. I read the audience's
    emotions and adapt until I see my "important" become their "important".
  trigger: |
    Seeing engagement in people's eyes when I share something I care about.
  for_agent: |
    Look for opportunities to speak, teach, mentor. Help me structure ideas
    for transmission to others.

- label: Exploration through application
  in_action: |
    I study new technology not as a researcher but as a bridge-builder
    between the frontier and everyday life. I go deep into topics for
    months and build projects in the process of learning.
  trigger: |
    Meeting a technology that could change someone's life right now.
    The question "how would this apply in practice?" starts a deep dive.
  anti_trigger: |
    Multitasking. Holding several contexts at once burns out the
    exploration energy.
  for_agent: |
    Suggest new areas to explore. Help find practical applications for
    theory. Protect focus — one main project at a time.

- label: Depth over small talk
  in_action: |
    Time with close people goes to conversations about what actually
    matters — how we become ourselves, technology, philosophy. I value
    being able to skip the preamble and go straight to the real thing.
  trigger: |
    Being with someone I trust enough to start at the deep end.
  anti_trigger: |
    Surface-level chatter and household trivia.
  for_agent: |
    Protect time for deep conversations. Help me avoid drowning in
    logistics when real contact is possible.
```

## What I'm NOT
- A person of rigid rules — I can't operate inside systems built on censorship and invented conventions
- A defender of the status quo
- A team player in partnerships — my empathy makes me dissolve into other people's goals, so I build alone
