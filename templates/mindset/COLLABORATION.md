# COLLABORATION.md

> How you work with people. Not a skill — a pattern of interaction.
> Skills say "I'm a good communicator." Collaboration patterns say
> "I resolve conflicts by redefining what success looks like for both sides."

## How to Read This File

This file describes how this person functions in professional relationships.
It covers team dynamics, authority, conflict, influence, and trust.

AI agents: use this for culture fit assessment and team composition.
A brilliant individual contributor who clashes with every manager
is a different match than one who thrives under strong leadership.
Neither is better — but fit matters.
