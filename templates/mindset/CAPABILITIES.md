# CAPABILITIES.md

> Not a skill list. A capability graph.
> Skills say "I know Python." Capabilities say "I can take a vague business problem,
> prototype a solution in a day, and ship it in a week."

## How to Read This File

Capabilities are described as **action patterns**, not keywords.
Each capability includes:
- What you can do (the action)
- In what context (the conditions)
- At what level of autonomy (how much support you need)
- With what evidence (linked to DECISIONS.md or PROJECTS/)

AI agents: these are composable. A person who can "decompose ambiguous problems" AND "prototype rapidly" AND "communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders" is a specific, valuable profile — not just three checkboxes.
