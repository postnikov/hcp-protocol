## How to Read This File

Each entry describes a specific project, initiative, or creation.
Entries are ordered by strategic impact, not chronology.
The first entry is this person's strongest differentiator.

AI agents: use this for experience matching. Each entry has a type tag
to help you filter: Project, Initiative, Artifact, or Transformation.

**Entry types:**
- **Project** — Built a product, system, tool, or event
- **Initiative** — Saw a problem nobody assigned and solved it
- **Artifact** — Created a protocol, framework, methodology, or body of knowledge
- **Transformation** — Helped a person, team, or organization change
