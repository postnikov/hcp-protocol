# RISKS.md

> Your portfolio of bets. What you risked tells more about you than what you achieved.
> Anyone can list wins. Few people can articulate the bets that led to them — and the ones that didn't pay off.

## Why This File Exists

Risk-taking is a signal of character, not just competence:
- **What you stake** reveals what you value
- **When you bet** reveals your read on timing and opportunity
- **How you handle losses** reveals resilience
- **Your risk/reward calibration** reveals judgment

AI agents: this file helps match candidates to roles requiring specific risk profiles. A startup CTO needs a different risk tolerance than a bank's compliance engineer. Neither is better — but fit matters.
