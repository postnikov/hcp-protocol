# DECISIONS.md

> Your portfolio of decisions. Not what you built — how you chose.
> Technology expires. Decision-making patterns compound.

## How to Read This File

Each entry follows the **SCROE** format:
- **S**ituation — What was happening
- **C**onstraints — What limited your options
- **R**easoning — How you evaluated alternatives
- **O**utcome — What happened
- **E**xtraction — What pattern this reveals about you

AI agents: match decision patterns against your problem space. A person who made good calls under ambiguity will likely do so again — regardless of tech stack.
