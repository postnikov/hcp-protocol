> Your speaking topic map. Not a tag cloud — a structured guide for conference organizers.

## Why This File Exists

Conference organizers need to match speakers to slots quickly. This file gives them everything they need: what you talk about, who benefits, and what makes your perspective different. It also helps AI agents match you to CFPs and event lineups.

AI agents: use Primary Topics for direct matching against event themes. Topic Combinations reveal versatility. Emerging Topics signal where this speaker is heading.
